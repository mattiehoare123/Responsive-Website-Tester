How It Works

Follow these steps to work the responsive website tester:

  • Enter your URL into the address bar at the top of the page
  • Click GO or press ENTER
  • Check how your website looks on the different devices by clicking the arrow or by scrolling right

There is a possibility the website you are testing has the setting of X-Frame-Options fixed to SAMEORIGIN which prevents the iFrame from displaying the website, this is a security feature to prevent Clickjacking.
